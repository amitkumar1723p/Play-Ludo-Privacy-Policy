Overview

Welcome to Play Ludo, a multiplayer board game developed and published by Webpage Walla. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary for gameplay:

  • Player Name: Provided by the user to display during online matches.
  • Room ID: User-entered identifier used to create or join multiplayer rooms.

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, device identifiers, or location data as part of standard gameplay.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to enable multiplayer functionality and a smooth gameplay experience:

  • Show player names to other participants in a game.
  • Create and manage multiplayer rooms using Room IDs.

We do not sell or rent personal information to third parties for marketing or other uses.

3. Advertisements & Third-Party Services

Play Ludo displays advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect non-personal information such as device identifiers and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads. For more details, please review Google’s privacy policy:

https://policies.google.com/privacy

If you use optional third-party features (for example, social sharing or analytics we add in the future), we will list those services here, and their privacy practices will govern that data separately.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security of any information submitted to the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

Play Ludo is not intended for users under the age of 13. If you believe your child has provided personal information without parental consent, please contact us at the address below and we will take steps to remove the information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app following notice of changes constitutes acceptance of those changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:
